### Description (4000 chars max)

Stop splitting rent down the middle when rooms aren't created equal.

Split Fair calculates each roommate's fair share of rent based on what their room actually offers — not just a flat 50/50 split that benefits whoever scored the better room.

**HOW IT WORKS**
Enter the total monthly rent, add each room, and rate it by size and features. Split Fair's scoring system turns every room into a number, then divides rent proportionally. Bigger room with a private bathroom? You pay more. Windowless cube next to the boiler? You pay less. That's just math.

**ROOM SCORING**
• Square footage — the foundation of every split
• Private bathroom +40 pts
• Parking spot +30 pts
• Balcony or patio +20 pts
• Walk-in closet +15 pts
• In-room A/C unit +10 pts
• Floor level bonus (up to +12 pts)
• Natural light rating (1–10)
• Quietness rating (1–10)
• Storage space rating (1–10)

**FEATURES**
• Support for 2 to 6 roommates
• Named rooms so everyone knows their number
• Visual scale showing relative room value at a glance
• Save configurations — when Tyler moves out and Priya moves in, reload and recalculate in seconds
• Share results via text, AirDrop, or any app
• Address field with history for quick re-entry

**PDF EXPORT ($1.99 one-time)**
Generate a professional, printable PDF of your full rent split with all room details included. One-time purchase. No subscription. Yours forever.

**SAVED CONFIGS ($1.99 one-time)**
Save unlimited room configurations so you can reload and recalculate whenever roommates change. One-time purchase — save as many setups as you need.

No accounts. No login. No data collected. Your roommates never have to know you used an app — just show up with a number and the math to back it up.

Split Fair: because "let's just split it evenly" is only fair when the rooms are too.
