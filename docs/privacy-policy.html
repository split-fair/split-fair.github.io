Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Split Fair is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device and is never sent to any server.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Split Fair (the "App"), developed by Nicolas Clairmont ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use our mobile application. Split Fair is a rent splitting calculator that helps users determine a fair division of rent based on room details such as size, features, and quality.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Split Fair does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any identifying information.

The App does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any other personally identifiable information

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide its core functionality, Split Fair stores certain data locally on your device using SharedPreferences (on Android) and equivalent local storage (on iOS). This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • Room configurations (size, features, quality ratings)
  • Rent amounts and calculated splits
  • Addresses associated with saved configurations
  • Saved configuration presets
  • In-app purchase status

You can clear this data at any time by clearing App data in your device settings or uninstalling the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Split Fair does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases: Split Fair offers two optional non-consumable in-app purchases ($1.99 each), processed entirely through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. All purchase transactions are handled by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Since we do not collect any data, there is nothing to share, sell, rent, or disclose to anyone.

6. Data Security

All data used by the App is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features. Since no data is transmitted over the internet, there is no risk of data interception during transmission.

7. Children's Privacy

Split Fair does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

8. Your Rights

Since Split Fair does not collect or store any personal data on servers, all data is stored locally on your device and is fully under your control. You can delete all App data at any time by clearing App data through your device settings or uninstalling the App.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
